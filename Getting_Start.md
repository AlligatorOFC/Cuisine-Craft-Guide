# 🍽️ Cuisine Craft – Getting Started

This guide will help you become a professional chef!

---

## 🧑‍🌾 Step 1 – Basic Survival

• Craft your first tools: sword, pickaxe, axe, shovel, etc.  
• Gather basic resources like wood and cobblestone  
• Collect crops such as:
  - Tomato 🍅  
  - Rice 🌾  
  - Green Onion 🌱  
  - Onion 🧅  
  - Chili Pepper 🌶️  

There are many crops to discover!

---

## 🍳 Step 2 – Cooking Table

• Craft the Cooking Table using:
  - 3 Slabs  
  - 4 Stones  

• The Cooking Table is used for:
  - Final recipes  
  - Complex meals  

👉 This will be your best friend!

---

## 🔪 Step 3 – Knife

• Craft the Knife using:
  - 1 Iron  
  - 1 Stick  

• You can use it as a weapon:
  - 3 Damage  
  - 150 Durability  

• Its main function is:
👉 Using it with the Cutting Board

---

## 🪵 Step 4 – Cooking Tools

### Cutting Board
• Used to cut ingredients and foods  
• Recipe:
  - 1 Knife  
  - 3 Slabs  

### Mixing Bowl
• Used to mix ingredients and make dough  
• Recipe:
  - 1 Shovel  
  - 1 Bowl
